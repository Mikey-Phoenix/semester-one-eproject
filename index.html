AboutUs
Alberto's Clocks and Watch Shop was established in Houston, Texas, 1983 and was aimed solely on watch and clock repairs. From there it grew to sales of thrift time pieces for the people of it's home state and now Alberto's Clocks and Watch Shop is one of the leading enterprises for clock and watch sales and repairs.
OUR PRODUCTS
Alberto's Clocks and Watch Shop is home to a wide range of clocks and time pieces, varrying from the most exotic and oestentatious products from some of the leading brands of clock and watch designers to the fairly used time pieces that are availale at affordable prices. Alberto's Clock and Watch Shop is your one stop destination for time pieces.
OUR SERVICES
At Alberto's Clock and Watch Shop, we not only put up the best time pieces for sale but also repair damaged time pieces at affordable prices. You can visit our workshop at 15103 Draper Rd, Houston, TX 77014, USA to hand over the item needing repairs or visit the Contact Us or Product Galary sections for more enquires.
